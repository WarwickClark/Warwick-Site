Sudoku Solver


This was one of my early projects. My father and I enjoy doing sudoku puzzles and competing against one another to see who is faster!

This pushed me to investigate if there was a way to automatically solve any sudoku puzzle, by simply adding in the given values, and eliminating the incorrect numbers. This lead me to backtracking.

After some research, trial and error, I was able to create a sudoku solver which solved any puzzle within just a few seconds. Although my father was proud, he also no longer wants to play sudoku with me anymore!
