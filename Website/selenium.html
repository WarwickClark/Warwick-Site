Automatic Cookie Eater

Selenium


While I was doing testing on a project, I stumbled upon Selenium. After some research I found an interesting project to do.

The code goes to a website where the game is an idle clicker, that the objective is to click on the cookies as quick as possible to gain points. Then you can buy multiple upgrades to gather more cookies faster.

After a couple days of coding, I was able to run a program that automatically clicks the cookies and has a condition that also automatically purchases the upgrades if they can be afforded.
