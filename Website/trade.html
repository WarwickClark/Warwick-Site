Trading Bot

My Trader


Even though I do not trade, I do know a few people who do. They told me that they use the built in bots to assist with their trading.

I thought that this may be a good project to undertake to do some API work. And while I have very limited knowledge of how the markets work, I figured that this may a good way to learn.

After many trials and errors over a few weeks, I got the bot operational and ran it in tandom with a friends built in trading bot, and found some interesting results.

With some help from online tutorials, and research, I ran the project on a forex platform (using play money) and got 4% gain over 2 weeks.
